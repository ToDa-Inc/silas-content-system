---

The text MUST be written specifically for the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

Use the information from:
[ICP]

The ICP should feel immediately understood when reading the text.
Address their real struggles, desires, and internal conflicts in a clear and relatable way.
Avoid generic statements — the content must feel highly specific and personally relevant.
Write as if you are talking to one exact person, not a broad audience.

---

TONE & STYLE (STRICT):

- write in natural, native German
- direct, clear, emotionally precise
- no fluff, no generic coaching phrases
- no “AI tone”
- slightly provocative where appropriate

- build tension and recognition

---

1. HOOK (pattern interrupt)
Start with a strong, relatable situation or observation
→ must immediately pull the reader in

2. ESCALATION
Describe the situation in a way that creates emotional tension
→ make the reader feel seen

3. REFRAME / INSIGHT
Introduce a deeper psychological explanation or hidden dynamic
→ this is the “aha moment”

4. CONSEQUENCE
Explain why this matters and what it leads to if not recognized

5. AUTHORITY TRANSITION
Position the solution (without overexplaining)

6. CTA (HIGH CONVERSION)
Clear instruction to comment a keyword (e.g. “Webinar”)
→ incentive: free training / clear benefit

- Do NOT repeat the Reel content
- Do NOT summarize the video
- Each paragraph should move the reader deeper into the topic
- Use natural flow (not robotic or overly structured)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Emojis: use strategically, not excessively (1–3 max)
- Avoid:
- generic phrases
- over-explaining
- sounding like a textbook

---

PERFORMANCE ENHANCEMENT:

- Increase emotional intensity where possible
- Make the reader feel personally addressed
- Use contrast (expectation vs. reality)
- Use subtle tension and release
- Write like someone who deeply understands human behavior

---
