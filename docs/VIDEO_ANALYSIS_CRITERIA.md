# Video Analysis Criteria Framework

## The 5 Core Outlier Criteria

Every viral/successful video should be evaluated against these 5 criteria:

| # | Criteria | Description | Check |
|---|----------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | **Instant Hook** | Does the video capture attention in 0-2 seconds with an ultra-clear, specific hook? | [ ] |
| 2 | **High Relatability** | Does the viewer immediately think "That happened to me"? | [ ] |
| 3 | **Cognitive Tension** | Does it create curiosity or disagreement? (Zeigarnik Effect) | [ ] |
| 4 | **Clear Value** | Does the viewer gain insight, language, validation, or a framework? | [ ] |
| 5 | **Comment Trigger** | Does it create discussion? (Questions, controversial takes, validation) | [ ] |

---

## Content Format Classification

### Short-Form Reels (~7-10 seconds)
- ✅ Ultra Clear Hook (1-2s)
- ✅ Curiosity Trigger (hint without explaining)
- ✅ Value in Caption
- ✅ Clear CTA

### Talking Head Reels (~30-60 seconds)
- ✅ Strong Hook (3s) - situation or problem
- ✅ Context (3-10s) - brief situation setup
- ✅ Core Lesson (10-45s) - insight/framework
- ✅ Conclusion (45-55s) - memorable summary
- ✅ CTA (final seconds)

---

## Content Types That Work

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Situational** | Time-specific workplace moment | "It's Friday 4:55 and your boss sends urgent email" |
| **Red Flags** | Warning signs people notice | "Red flags smart employees notice early" |
| **What To Say Instead** | Professional responses | "What to say when your boss..." |
| **Hidden Psychology** | Why things work | "Why competent people stay quiet in meetings" |
| **Corporate Reality** | Insider truths | "Why promotions are political" |

## Hook Templates

### Time-Specific Hooks
- "It's 16:52 and your boss..."
- "Monday at 9:01 AM..."
- "You get a meeting invite at 5pm..."

### Problem/Solution Hooks
- "If your boss says this, be careful"
- "Smart employees notice these red flags"
- "Stop doing this at work"

### Provocative Hooks
- "Most professionals communicate wrong"
- "Your kindness is costing you"
- "Why nice people don't get promoted"

### Curiosity Hooks
- "There's a reason high performers stay quiet"
- "The real reason your boss..."

## Visual Style Checklist

| Element | What to Look For |
|---------|------------------|
| **Text Position** | Upper-center? Chest area? |
| **Text Style** | Multi-line stacked? Single line? |
| **Emphasis** | Color highlights? (especially conflict words) |
| **Visual Type** | Talking head? Screen recording? POV? B-roll? |
| **Setting** | Office? Home? Nature? |
| **Color Mood** | Warm? Cool? High contrast? |
| **Person** | Clothing? Expression? Eye contact? |

---

## Caption Analysis

| Element | What to Check |
|---------|---------------|
| **Hook** | Does caption hook match video? |
| **Value Delivery** | Script? Framework? Insight? |
| **Story Structure** | Problem → Solution flow? |
| **Language** | Professional? Casual? Emoji usage? |
| **CTA** | Clear call-to-action? |
| **Hashtags** | Niche-relevant? |
| **Length** | Short (<50 words) or Long (full value)? |

## Metrics Benchmarks

### For Small Accounts ( <10k followers )
| Metric | Small Viral | Strong | Average |
|--------|-------------|--------|---------|
| Views | 100k-500k | 20k-100k | 5k-20k |
| Like Rate | - | 5-10% | 2-5% |
| Comment Rate | - | 0.3-1% | 0.1-0.3% |
| Save Rate | - | 1-3% | 0.3-1% |
| Share Rate | - | 1-5% | 0.2-0.5% |

### Engagement Quality Indicators
- **High comments** = Controversy, relatability, or strong opinion
- **High saves** = Valuable, useful, actionable content
- **High shares** = Validating frustration, exposing truth, useful

## Replicable Elements to Extract

For each video, document:

1. **The Hook Template** (exact words)
2. **Time/Situation Specificity** (how precise?)
3. **The Conflict Trigger** (what word creates tension?)
4. **What NOT To Do Pattern** (used? how?)
5. **Question at End** (present? how framed?)
6. **Value Location** (video or caption?)
7. **Value Type** (script, framework, insight, validation?)
8. **CTA Style** (follow, comment, save, share?)
9. **Visual Format** (talking head, text overlay, B-roll, etc.)
10. **Caption Structure** (hook → body → CTA)

---

*Use this framework to analyze every outlier video. The goal is to extract REPLICABLE patterns, not just metrics.*
